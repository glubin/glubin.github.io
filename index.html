Project Based Student Coding

We are Wash U students teaching other Wash U students the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and how to use available resources to build their own websites and applications. Groups of three students and their mentor work collaborate on a semester-long project of their choice that allows them to apply what they learn in a practical way. Feel free to peruse the tools and resources on our Resources page to get started on your own projects, to check out groups' projects from past semesters, or to reach out to us with any questions on our Contact Us page.
