about us



Who we are

Here at HelloWorld, we are a group of WashU students passionate about learning and expanding our knowledge about the coding world. Our club founder, Greg Lubin, enjoys camels, salmon, and leaving horses in people's bedrooms every once in a while. One day, he will also own a restaurant called Salmon Ella. Assisting Greg in his teaching endeavors are Sam Shields and Ross, both coding experts and computer science students at WashU. The rest of the members of HelloWorld included Emily Orr, Alex Gould, Michelle Shtivelberg, Matt Weinberg, Sam Guenin, and Vic Gainor- all participants who learned a lot about coding over the course of the semester.

What we do

This semester, we decided to divide into two groups of students, each with different levels of coding background, to each work on an exciting, individualized project. Over the course of the semester, we learned basic skills in coding languages such as HTML, CSS, as Javascript, in addition to learning important lingo, processes, and programs in the coding world such as Terminal and Bootstrap. Through lecture-style and collaborative meetings, each group was able to work together to design awesome projects, such as this website and a music app page. Check out our Past Projects page for more details!
